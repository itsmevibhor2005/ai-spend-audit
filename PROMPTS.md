# Gemini Prompt

Used only for personalized audit summary.

Prompt:

Generate a short personalized AI spend audit summary.

Include:

- total savings
- biggest opportunity
- recommendation

Tone:

Professional and concise.

Fallback:

If Gemini fails, return a template summary.

## Why this prompt

Kept short to reduce latency and cost.

## What I tried

Longer prompts produced repetitive text.

Short structured prompts worked better.
