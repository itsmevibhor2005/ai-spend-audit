# Interview 1

**Name:** A. (3rd year student, IIT Kanpur)  
**Role:** Building a side project for hackathons

### Direct quotes

> “I use ChatGPT Plus and GitHub Copilot, but I’ve honestly never checked if I’m overlapping.”

> “Most months I just pay and move on because the amount feels small.”

> “If a tool instantly told me what to cancel or downgrade, I’d use it.”

### Most surprising thing

He did not know that some tools offer team pricing or enterprise pricing that can be cheaper than stacking individual subscriptions.

### What it changed

I added **Benchmark Mode** so users can compare their spend against similar teams and quickly understand if they are overpaying.

# Interview 2

**Name:** R. (Hackathon teammate)  
**Role:** Student developer using ChatGPT + Claude during projects

### Direct quotes

> “If you ask for my email before showing results, I’ll probably leave.”

> “The main thing I care about is instant output.”

> “If I see useful numbers first, I’m okay giving my email later.”

### Most surprising thing

He strongly preferred seeing the audit first before any signup or lead capture.

### What it changed

I moved **email capture after showing value** and made the audit accessible instantly without login.

# Interview 3

**Name:** S. (Senior at IIT Kanpur)  
**Role:** Internship + freelance projects

### Direct quotes

> “A shareable report link would be useful when discussing tooling with teammates.”

> “The cost numbers are more convincing when they’re visual.”

> “A founder would probably forward this to someone else before deciding.”

### Most surprising thing

He cared more about sharing and discussing the report than the raw pricing numbers.

### What it changed

I added:

- **Shareable public audit URL**
- **Copy link button**
- **Charts + benchmark cards**

to make the report easier to forward and discuss.
